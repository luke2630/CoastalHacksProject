When is the best time to exercise?

There are different benefits to exercising at different times of day. Working out in the morning are the best way to lose weight. It may also shift your body clock earlier making it easier to wake up in the morning. Working out in the afternoon give the best performance boost. It can also shift your body clock the same way a morning workout does. Nightime workouts can reduce feeling hungry. This could help with reducing weight gain. This is according to Time Magazine. Due to the fact that most people are unable to exercise for an hour or more at a time, 10-15 minute workouts are usually all people can fit in.
