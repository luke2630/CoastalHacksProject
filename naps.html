Naps

When is the best time to nap?

Naps should take place after lunch. This is a period known as the post-lunch dip. This is the time someone is most likely to feel sleepy. However, they should not take place after 2pm since this will impair one's ability to fall asleep at a reasonable time later in the night. This is according to recommendations from sleep.org. How long should naps be?

Ideally power naps should last 10 minutes. Studies show that naps that are shorter provide little to no benefits over not napping and those that are longer often result in a state of drowsiness afterwards known as "sleep inertia." This recommendation is based on data from Amber Brooks, PHD and Leon Lack, PHD from the Sleep Research Society. Found here: link
