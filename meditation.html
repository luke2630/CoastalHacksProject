Meditation

When is the best time to meditate?

Experts have not conclusively decided when the best time to meditate is. It often varies from person to person. Listed below is an example of a time slots recommended by Mindworks. Link found here: link

  • Morning
  • The benefits of meditating in the morning are that since the mind is fresh it is easier to remain calm. It also sets a productive tone for the rest of the day and helps prepare one for the day.

Are short meditation bursts still effective?

Even meditating for a few minutes can be beneficial. It can provide a sense of calm and peace. According to the Mayo Clinic there are many, many benefits to meditating. Including building stress management, increasing self-awareness, reducing negative emotions and more. It may also help those with a medical condition, especially those worsened by stresses.
